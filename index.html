Startup Commons

Growth and success of startups in Kerala is our first priority. Kerala Startup Mission in keeping with the vision of the state government regarding encouraging startups in various sectors would like to plan to set up Startup Commons which shall provide all vital information about the basic requirements to start a company and help startups to move forward. Startup Commons is an effort by Kerala Startup Mission to onboard a collective of agencies and individuals who are willing to support startups during its various phases of growth right from inception to IPO.

How to apply

In addition to the documents mentioned in the above section, applicants should submit the following documents with their application. Performance against the parameters must be duly supported with self-attested documentary evidence.
  1. Profile of the Agency.
  2. Details of services offered.
  3. Organization setup in terms of staff indicating their qualifications and competencies.
  4. Major achievements.
  5. Self-Certificate stating that the agency has not been blacklisted in the past.
  6. A certificate indicating that information submitted by him/her is correct. Anyone found guilty of furnishing false information shall be blacklisted by the Kerala Startup Mission.
  7. Applicant agencies must furnish a copy of their GSTIN Registration certificate/Aadhaar.
  8. Service Satisfaction Certificate from any of the five clients needs to be submitted.

Award of work

The agencies selected will be empanelled as service provider under the Startup Commons of Kerala Startup Mission. After onboarding the agencies KSUM shall arrive at a fixed price for providing services to startups.
